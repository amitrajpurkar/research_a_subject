## Chapter 1 — Economics and Life

### Core concepts
Scarcity, opportunity cost, marginal analysis, trade-offs, incentives, rational behaviour, efficiency.

### Resources

- [**Khan Academy — Basic Economic Concepts (Macroeconomics)**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/ap-macroeconomics/basic-economics-concepts-macro) — Full unit with videos and exercises on scarcity, opportunity cost, and PPC
- [**Quizlet — Karlan/Morduch Chapter 1 Key Terms**](https://quizlet.com/72573593/macroeconomics-karlanmorduch-chapter-1-key-terms-flash-cards/) — Flashcards built directly from this textbook
- [**Quizlet — Module 1: Foundations of Economics (Chapters 1–4)**](https://quizlet.com/972608598/module-1-foundations-of-economics-quiz-chapters-1-4-flash-cards/) — Practice quiz covering the first block of topics
- [**Quizlet — ECO 2013 Exam 1 Flashcards**](https://quizlet.com/6548218/eco-2013-exam-1-flash-cards/) — Flashcard set specific to ECO 2013 Exam 1
- [**Quizlet — ECO2013 Macroeconomics Exam 1**](https://quizlet.com/514244648/eco2013-macroeconomics-exam-1-flash-cards/) — Another ECO 2013 set; covers Chapters 1–5
- [**CliffsNotes — Scarcity, Opportunity Cost, Marginal Analysis (worked problem)**](https://www.cliffsnotes.com/tutors-problems/Macroeconomics/47791544--1-Scarcity-opportunity-cost-and-marginal-analysis-Hilary-is/) — Worked example explaining how to apply these concepts

### Key terms to know
Scarcity, opportunity cost, marginal cost, marginal benefit, trade-off, incentive, efficiency, equity, rational behaviour, production possibilities curve (PPC)

## Chapter 2 — Specialization and Exchange

### Core concepts
Comparative advantage, absolute advantage, gains from trade, specialisation, the production possibilities frontier (PPF).

### Resources

- [**Lumen Learning — Comparative Advantage and Gains from Trade**](https://courses.lumenlearning.com/wm-macroeconomics/chapter/reading-absolute-advantage-2/) — Clear, free textbook-style explanations with examples
- [**OpenStax Macroeconomics 2e — Absolute and Comparative Advantage**](https://courses.lumenlearning.com/suny-fmcc-macroeconomics/chapter/absolute-and-comparative-advantage/) — Full chapter with Saudi Arabia/US trade examples
- [**Pearson — PPF, Comparative Advantage, and Trade (video + practice)**](https://www.pearson.com/channels/macroeconomics/learn/brian/ch-2-introductory-economic-models/ppf-comparative-advantage-and-trade) — Video lessons with practice exercises; good for the PPF graphs
- [**CSUN — Comparative Advantage Explained (PDF)**](https://www.csun.edu/sites/default/files/micro2.pdf) — Concise academic explainer; good for checking your understanding
- [**Studocu — Chapter 2: Specialization and Exchange Notes**](https://www.studocu.com/en-us/document/the-university-of-texas-at-san-antonio/microeconomics/microeconomics-ch2-specialization-and-exchange/30530922) — Lecture notes from another university covering the same Karlan/Morduch material

### Key terms to know
Absolute advantage, comparative advantage, opportunity cost of production, specialisation, gains from trade, production possibilities frontier (PPF), imports, exports

## Chapter 3 — Markets

### Core concepts
Demand, supply, market equilibrium, price mechanism, shifts in demand and supply curves, price floors, price ceilings, market efficiency.

### Resources

- [**Khan Academy — Supply, Demand, and Market Equilibrium**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/microeconomics/supply-demand-equilibrium) — The definitive free resource for this topic; videos, exercises, and articles
- [**Khan Academy — Changes in Equilibrium Price and Quantity (video)**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/ap-macroeconomics/basic-economics-concepts-macro/market-equilibrium-disequilibrium-and-changes-in-equilibrium/v/changes-in-equilibrium-price-and-quantity-when-supply-and-demand-change-khan-academy) — Walks through what happens to equilibrium when supply or demand shifts
- [**Khan Academy — AP Macroeconomics: Supply and Demand (YouTube playlist)**](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLSQl0a2vh4HAdWBHe3mblDiDRAUNEyC-F) — Full playlist; watch at 1.25× speed
- [**Pearson Macroeconomics — Aggregate Supply and Demand Study Guide**](https://www.pearson.com/channels/macroeconomics/study-guides/aggregate-supply-and-aggregate-demand-macroeconomic-2) — Study guide format; useful for graphing practice
- [**PSU Pressbooks — Supply and Demand Companion Chapter**](https://psu.pb.unizin.org/introductiontomacroeconomicscompanion/chapter/chapter-3-supply-and-demand/) — Open-access university textbook chapter with practice questions
- [**Khan Academy — Market Equilibrium Exercise**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/ap-microeconomics/unit-2-supply-and-demnd/26/e/market-equilibrium-and-price-controls) — Interactive exercise with price controls

### Key terms to know
Law of demand, law of supply, equilibrium price, equilibrium quantity, surplus, shortage, demand shifters, supply shifters, price ceiling, price floor, consumer surplus, producer surplus

## Chapter 7 — Measuring GDP

### Core concepts
Gross Domestic Product, the expenditure approach (C + I + G + NX), the income approach, nominal vs. real GDP, GDP deflator, circular flow model, limitations of GDP.

### Resources

- [**Khan Academy — Measuring the Size of the Economy: GDP**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/macroeconomics/macro-economic-indicators-and-the-business-cycle/macro-the-circular-flow-and-gdp/a/measuring-the-size-of-the-economy-gross-domestic-product-cnx) — Full reading with the circular flow model
- [**Khan Academy — Parsing GDP (video)**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/ap-macroeconomics/economic-iondicators-and-the-business-cycle/21/v/parsing-gross-domestic-product) — Best video introduction to breaking down the GDP formula
- [**Khan Academy — GDP: Measuring National Income (YouTube playlist)**](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLSQl0a2vh4HB-eppLyKiTFRfPXBMQ76OZ) — Full playlist covering GDP, real vs. nominal, deflator
- [**Khan Academy — Limitations of GDP (video)**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/ap-macroeconomics/economic-iondicators-and-the-business-cycle/limitations-of-gdp/v/limitations-of-gdp-ap-macroeconomics-khan-academy) — What GDP misses; useful for exam short-answer questions
- [**Khan Academy — Examples of Accounting for GDP (video)**](https://www.khanacademy.org/economics-finance-domain/macroeconomics/macro-economic-indicators-and-the-business-cycle/macro-the-circular-flow-and-gdp/v/examples-of-accounting-for-gdp) — Worked examples; essential for the numerical questions on exams
- [**PSU Pressbooks — Gross Domestic Product Chapter**](https://pressbooks.pub/introtomacrosc/chapter/gross-domestic-product/) — Open-access; clean chapter-level treatment
- [**CORE Economics — Measuring GDP**](https://books.core-econ.org/the-economy/macroeconomics/03-aggregate-demand-02-gross-domestic-product.html) — University-level open textbook; deeper than Karlan for optional reading

### Key terms to know
GDP, expenditure approach (C + I + G + NX), income approach, nominal GDP, real GDP, GDP deflator, circular flow, intermediate goods (excluded from GDP), final goods, net exports

## Chapter 8 — The Cost of Living

### Core concepts
Consumer Price Index (CPI), inflation, deflation, real vs. nominal values, purchasing power, substitution bias, the basket of goods.

### Resources

- [**Fiveable — How to Measure Changes in the Cost of Living**](https://fiveable.me/principles-macroeconomics/unit-9/2-measure-cost-living/study-guide/6oILkIQHlzkjrQHN) — Study guide format; concise and exam-focused

### Key terms to know
CPI, inflation rate, deflation, purchasing power, nominal vs. real values, basket of goods, substitution bias, GDP deflator vs. CPI (difference), base year, price index formula

### Core concepts
Types of unemployment (frictional, structural, cyclical), the natural rate of unemployment, the labor force participation rate, the Phillips Curve.

### Resources

### Key terms to know
Frictional unemployment, structural unemployment, cyclical unemployment, natural rate of unemployment (NRU), labor force participation rate, underemployed, discouraged workers, Phillips Curve, NAIRU

## Chapter 11 — Aggregate Expenditure & Economic Fluctuations

### Core concepts
Aggregate expenditure (AE) model, consumption function, multiplier effect, business cycles, recession, expansion, the role of investment.

### Resources

- [**PSU Pressbooks — The Aggregate Expenditure Model**](https://psu.pb.unizin.org/introductiontomacroeconomics/chapter/the-aggregate-expenditure-model/) — Open-access university chapter; clean and clear

### Key terms to know
Aggregate expenditure, consumption function, marginal propensity to consume (MPC), multiplier, autonomous spending, business cycle, recession, expansion, trough, peak

M1, M2, fractional reserve banking, reserve requirement, money multiplier (= 1/reserve ratio), open market operations, federal funds rate, expansionary monetary policy, contractionary monetary policy, lender of last resort
